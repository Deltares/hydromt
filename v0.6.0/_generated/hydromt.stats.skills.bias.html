hydromt.stats.skills.bias#

hydromt.stats.skills.bias(sim, obs, dim='time')[source]#

Returns the bias between two time series.

\[Bias=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}(obs_{i}-sim_{i})\]
Parameters:

  • sim (xarray DataArray) – simulations time series

  • obs (xarray DataArray) – observations time series

  • dim (str, optional) – name of time dimension in sim and obs (the default is ‘time’)

Returns:

bias

Return type:

xarray DataArray
